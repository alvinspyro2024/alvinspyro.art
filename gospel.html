The Gospel Message

John MacArthur, a well-known pastor and Bible teacher, emphasizes a clear, biblical, and direct presentation of the gospel. While he doesn’t use a rigid step-by-step formula like some evangelistic programs, he has described his one-on-one evangelism approach as:

  1. Starting with God's Holiness and the Law: God is holy. We’ve sinned (Romans 3:23). The Ten Commandments show our guilt.
  2. Presenting the Person and Work of Christ:
    • Jesus is fully God and fully man
    • Lived a sinless life
    • Died as our substitute (Isaiah 53:5–6, 2 Corinthians 5:21)
    • Rose from the dead
  3. Calling for Repentance and Faith: Not “easy-believism,” but true repentance and belief in Jesus (Mark 1:15).
  4. Avoiding Manipulation: No scripted prayers—just genuine heart response.
  5. Pointing to Scripture and Discipleship: Encourage reading John or Romans and joining a Bible-teaching church.

Simplified Version of MacArthur’s Gospel Sharing

  1. God’s Holiness: He created us to live for Him.
  2. Man’s Sinfulness: We’ve all sinned—lied, lusted, been selfish.
  3. Consequence: Sin deserves eternal separation—hell.
  4. Christ’s Work: Jesus died in our place and rose again.
  5. Faith & Repentance: Trust Jesus, turn from sin.
  6. Heart Response: Surrender to Him—not religion, but relationship.

Narrative: Sharing the Gospel One-on-One

Setting: On a flight from L.A. to Dallas. MacArthur speaks with a businessman named Mike.

MacArthur: “Mike, can I ask—what do you think happens after we die?”

Mike: “Hopefully a better place.”

MacArthur: “What if God told us?”

Mike: “Heaven or hell?”

MacArthur: “Yes. God is holy. We’ve all sinned. Sin must be judged.”

Mike: “I’ve messed up.”

MacArthur: “Jesus lived perfectly, died for sinners, rose again. Repent and trust Him.”

Mike: “Do I need to pray something?”

MacArthur: “Speak from your heart. If God’s drawing you, respond.”

MacArthur: “Start reading the Gospel of John.”

Memorizable Outline (G.R.A.C.E.D.)

  1. G – God is holy: He made us to know Him.
  2. R – Rebellion is our problem: We’ve all sinned.
  3. A – Accountability is real: Sin brings death.
  4. C – Christ is the solution: He died and rose again.
  5. E – Exchange through repentance: Trust and turn to Him.
  6. D – Decision is personal: Talk to God sincerely.

Sample Script (Natural Style)

“Bro, can I share something that changed my life?

God is holy. He made us for Him. But we’ve sinned—lied, lusted, turned away.

Because He’s just, sin must be judged. That’s why hell is real.

But Jesus came, lived perfectly, died, and rose again.

God calls us to turn from sin and trust in Jesus—not religion, but a real relationship.

If your heart is ready, talk to Him. No fancy words. He hears you.”
