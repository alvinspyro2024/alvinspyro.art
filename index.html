About Me

I’m Alvin, based in Las Piñas City, Philippines. As a retired international airline crew member, I’ve traveled extensively and been inspired by different cultures and artistic traditions. I first discovered pyrographic art abroad and was fascinated by its beauty and depth. What started as a hobby of laser photo engraving soon became a ministry—engraving Bible verses on wood as spiritual reminders. My wife is my encouragement, and every piece I make is prayerfully crafted from the heart, to reflect that Jesus is the way, the truth, and the life.
